Koffee Karma is about giving. At the tap of a button, you can brighten up a random user's day - and your own - by sending them an Offering. What's an Offering? It's a $2.10 ticket that's redeemable for a cup of coffee. If you want, in the time it takes to write a Tweet (or less) you can include a message to personalize your gift as well. Our goal at Koffee Karma is to give everyone an easy, fun and fast way to experience the gift of giving every day.

Simply for signing up as a user of Koffee Karma you're eligible to receive one Offering. That's not all - every Offering you send enables you to get one more. Maybe while you're sleeping, maybe during your commute, maybe at the end of a rough day, or even as you wait in line at the coffee shop you might be surprised by a new Offering!

Join Koffee Karma and never simply buy coffee again. Turn that common, everyday action into an opportunity to make someone smile instead. Get some good karma by sending out an Offering, and turn in one of your own at the shop.
